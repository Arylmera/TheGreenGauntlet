Drop CC0 / public-domain sound effects in this directory:

  coin.ogg    — short "coin pickup" chime, ~200ms, -6 dB peak
  bounce.ogg  — soft bounce / bump, ~300ms

Recommended source: https://kenney.nl/assets/interface-sounds (CC0)
or https://freesound.org (filter by Creative Commons 0).

DO NOT use ripped Nintendo SFX — those are trademarked.

Until files are dropped in, useSound() fails silently (no playback,
no console errors). The rest of the UI works unchanged.
